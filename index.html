Our Core Services to Keep Your Website Safe and Operational

Our comprehensive suite of services ensures your website is always secure, optimized, and running smoothly. From real-time monitoring and routine maintenance to advanced cybersecurity and recovery solutions, we provide everything you need to safeguard your online presence.

Request a Free Website Audit

Website Monitoring

Real-time tracking of your website’s uptime, performance, and security using cutting-edge monitoring technologies. Stay informed and proactive with immediate alerts for any issues.

  • Real-time Uptime Monitoring with Prometheus and Grafana
    Monitor your website’s availability 24/7 with Prometheus data collection and Grafana’s visually rich dashboards for instant insights.
  • Performance Tracking to Ensure Optimal Loading Speed
    Track response times, resource utilization, and server performance metrics to keep your website running smoothly.
  • Proactive Issue Detection
    Identify trends and anomalies before they escalate into critical problems, using advanced alerting mechanisms in Prometheus.
  • Detailed Reports on Website Performance and Availability
    Gain actionable insights with customized reports generated from Grafana and the ELK stack, showcasing uptime, performance metrics, and error logs.
  • Comprehensive Log Analysis with ELK Stack
    Centralize, analyze, and visualize logs from servers and applications with Elasticsearch, Logstash, and Kibana to quickly diagnose and resolve issues.
  • Alerting and Notifications
    Receive real-time alerts via email, Slack, or other integrated platforms when your website faces performance or availability issues.

Website Maintenance & Backup

Routine updates, optimizations, and reliable backups ensure your website remains secure, operational, and recoverable, leveraging the power of AWS infrastructure.

  • High-Performance Hosting with AWS EC2 / Lightsail
    Your website will be hosted on AWS EC2 or Lightsail, ensuring reliability, scalability, and performance with globally distributed infrastructure.
  • Scheduled Backups to AWS S3
    Regular backups of website files and databases are securely stored in AWS S3, offering durability and quick access when needed.
  • Long-Term Archival with AWS S3 Glacier
    Critical backups are safely archived in AWS S3 Glacier for long-term retention, ensuring cost-effective and highly durable storage.
  • Routine Updates and Optimization
    CMS, plugins, and themes are updated regularly to prevent vulnerabilities, while databases and files are optimized for faster performance.
  • Disaster Recovery Planning
    With automated backups and quick restoration options via AWS services, your website can be restored quickly in case of any issues.
  • Monitoring and Resource Optimization
    Continuous tracking of hosting performance and resource usage ensures cost efficiency and smooth operation.

Website Recovery from Hacking

Expert-driven solutions to recover your website from cyberattacks, restore functionality, and secure your online presence. Our comprehensive service includes post-recovery monitoring and penetration testing for ongoing protection.

  • Manual Recovery by a Professional Developer
    Our experienced developers meticulously remove malware, backdoors, and unauthorized scripts, restoring your website to its original functionality.
  • Investigation and Root Cause Analysis
    Identify how the hack occurred, patch vulnerabilities, and secure potential entry points to prevent future incidents.
  • Full Security Audit Post-Recovery
    A thorough review of your website’s code, configurations, and server environment to ensure a clean and secure state.
  • Continuous Monitoring and Penetration Testing
    For 3-6 months, we monitor your website and perform periodic penetration testing to identify and address potential security gaps.
  • Detailed Recovery Report
    Receive a comprehensive report detailing the recovery process, security measures applied, and recommendations for maintaining site security.
